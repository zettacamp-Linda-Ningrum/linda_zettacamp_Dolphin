Hello, Swifties!

   SWIFT4U is a site that provides everything about the work or the discography of a Taylor Swift. Where this includes
single songs, and album packages made by Taylor Swift. This site is made especially for you, Taylor Swift fans, or what
we usually call ourselves as a Swifties. So, this website will provide an update service regarding information from Taylor Swift's discrography releases, both albums and singles. The information provided includes:
  • The description of the album or single;
  • Album tracklist;
  • Preview of the album with a little snippet of the song;
  • as well as the price of the album.
   This SWIFT4U website was created specifically for Swifties to make it easier for fans to find out the latest information from Taylor Swift's discography, without the need to look for it on many other websites, because this website is a collection of information available on other websites which also directly quotes information from the official Taylor Swift website. Apart from the official website, the sources used for information on this website are also based on Taylor Swift's official music platforms, such as Spotify, Apple Music, Shazam, and others.
