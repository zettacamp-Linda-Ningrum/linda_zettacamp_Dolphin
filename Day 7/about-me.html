BOO(k)DE

BOO(k)DE is an online bookstore that focuses on providing fast and quality books. Supported by the many publishers that have collaborated, BOO(k)DE is able to present the latest and complete catalog from each publisher. In addition, BOO(k)DE also provides fast and reliable book delivery services.

We are here to serve Indonesian book lovers since August 11 2023. BOO(k)DE started as a group of young and creative people with IT backgrounds who are involved in the world of education and e-commerce in Indonesia.

Why online bookstores? Because we are aware, books are a source of knowledge. Because we understand, not everyone has time to go to a bookstore.

Through BOO(k)DE, you can place orders 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We provide new books and provide delivery services throughout Indonesia.
