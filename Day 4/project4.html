TOP 4 MOVIE BASED ON BOOKS ADAPTATION

There are lots of novels with a variuos genre in this world. Not a few of these novels were also adapted into a film. However, from a number of novel adaptation films that I have watched, there are 4 of them which are my favorites. So, the following four best films are based on my preferences.

Paper Towns

Paper Towns is a 2015 American romantic mystery drama film directed by Jake Schreier and produced by Marty Bowen and Wyck Godfrey. The script was written by Scott Neustadter and Michael H. Weber based on the novel Paper Towns by John Green.

The Fault in Our Stars

The Fault in Our Stars is a 2014 American romantic drama film directed by Josh Boone and produced by Marty Bowen and Wyck Godfrey. The screenplay was written by Scott Neustadter and Michael H. Weber based on the novel The Fault in Our Stars by John Green.

Enola Holmes

Enola Holmes is a 2020 British mystery film based on the first book in the series of the same name by Nancy Springer. Telling the story of the teenage sister of Sherlock Holmes, the film was directed by Harry Bradbeer and written by Jack Thorne.

Little Women

Little Women is a 2019 American coming-of-age period drama film written and directed by Greta Gerwig. It is the seventh film adaptation of the 1868 novel of the same name by Louisa May Alcott. It chronicles the lives of the March sisters—Meg, Jo, Beth, and Amy—in Concord, Massachusetts, during the 19th century.
